ORDER & DELIVERY PROCEDURES

Order Procedure

Through our Official Website:

  1. You can get mysterious discounts in your next purchase when you participate in our quiz. Click the to participate & win!!!
  2. Browse our products to find the products you love.
  3. Select the items you want to purchase and add them to your cart.
  4. Once you have finished selecting your items, go to your cart and proceed to checkout.
  5. Fill the delivery information and choose your preferred payment method.
  6. Review your order and submit it. You will receive a confirmation email with the details of your order.

  • A delivery charge will be added to the final pricing. The delivery charge may change at the discretion of CMS Bakery.

  • CMS Bakery will bear no liability for any failure or delay in delivery arisen out of circumstances beyond their control.
