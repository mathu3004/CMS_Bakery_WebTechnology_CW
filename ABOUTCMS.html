ABOUT CMS

Mission Statement

At CMS Bakery Shop, our mission is to craft high-quality, freshly baked goods that bring joy to our customers. We are committed to using the finest ingredients and traditional baking techniques to create delicious products that reflect our passion for baking and dedication to excellence.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to be the premier bakery in our community, known for our exceptional quality, innovation, and customer service. We aim to expand our reach while maintaining our artisanal approach, ensuring that each customer enjoys a unique and delightful bakery experience.

Our Team

Christine Matthews (Founder and Head Baker)


Christine has over 20 years of baking experience and a passion for creating delicious, high-quality baked goods. She founded CMS Bakery Shop to share her love for baking with the community and is dedicated to maintaining the artisanal standards that define our bakery.

Mark Johnson (General Manager)


Mark oversees the daily operations of CMS Bakery Shop, ensuring everything runs smoothly. With a background in hospitality management, he focuses on providing excellent customer service and a welcoming atmosphere for all our visitors.

Sarah Lee (Pastry Chef)


Sarah brings creativity and precision to our pastry offerings. A graduate of a prestigious culinary school, she is always experimenting with new recipes and techniques to delight our customers with innovative and delectable pastries.

John Davis (Marketing and Community Outreach Coordinator)


John is responsible for promoting CMS Bakery Shop and organizing community events. His efforts ensure that our bakery remains an integral part of the local community, helping us connect with our customers and give back through various initiatives.

History

CMS Bakery Shop was founded in 2000 by Christine Matthews, who transformed her passion for baking into a thriving business. Starting with a single storefront in a charming neighborhood, the bakery quickly became a favorite among locals for its commitment to quality and tradition.

As word spread about our delicious baked goods, CMS Bakery Shop expanded its offerings to include a wide variety of breads, pastries, cakes, and seasonal specialties. Despite our growth, we have remained true to our roots, using traditional methods and the best ingredients to ensure the highest quality in every bite.

Over the years, we have expanded to multiple locations, each maintaining the warm and welcoming atmosphere of our original shop. We have also introduced a line of gluten-free and vegan products to cater to the diverse preferences of our customers.

Our bakery is not just a business, but a part of the community. We participate in local farmers' markets, sponsor community events, and collaborate with local schools and charities. We believe in giving back and fostering strong community relationships.

Today, CMS Bakery Shop is a beloved local institution, known not only for our exceptional products but also for our active involvement in the community. We are proud to support local events and charities, and we look forward to continuing to serve our community with the same passion and dedication that has defined us from the beginning.
